ABOUT US

Company

An importer of industrial components supporting Japanese manufacturing since 1955.

Contributing to our customers' manufacturing and to a richer future for society.

Since our founding in 1955, K.MECS ONE has grown together with Japanese manufacturing by delivering world-class industrial components from around the globe. As an authorized distributor of MOXA, ODU and POSITAL, we combine premium products with certified engineering support.

We aim to be a technical partner that solves our customers' real challenges — not just a parts supplier.
